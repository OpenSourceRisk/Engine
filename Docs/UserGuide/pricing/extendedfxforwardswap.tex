\subsubsection{Extended Fx Forward Swap}
\label{pricing:extendedfxforwardswap}
 
An Extended Fx Forward Swap is an exchange of notionals at multiple times in the future. We buy B and sell A
$$
\NPV= \sum N \div K - N \div X_A(T)
$$
Where:
\begin{itemize}
\item $N$: the notional amount in currency A
\item $K$: the strike rate
\item $X_A(T)$: the FX spot rate between currencies A and base currency at time T
\end{itemize}
